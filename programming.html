Wolfhouse is a BIPOC-driven incubator for forward-thinking art & design across physical and digital mediums. Wolfhouse is committed to elevating the pioneering voices that shape culture today.

The Wolfhouse residence acts as the nexus of energy for physical activations & initiatives. This includes exhibitions, events and lectures, brand partnerships and staging, filming, and residencies.

The Wolfhouse digital program supports commissions and collaborations in web3 including a VR gallery, NFT drops, and metaverse events centered around art, design, architecture, and digital real estate.
